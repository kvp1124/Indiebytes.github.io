Paneer

Yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chill, corriander, garam masala, Kasuri methi, lime juice, and salt should all be combined in a sizable mixing basin. All of it has been thoroughly combined. To the bowl, add paneer, bell peppers, and onions. Toss everything together thoroughly to coat. Enjoy after a short time in the oven!

Time 90 mins

Tofu Mix

Place the shiitake mushrooms in water, then discard the water. It is necessary for the sauce. To eliminate extra water, squeeze the dried shiitake mushrooms, then slice them finely. Bring them and the button mushrooms together in a basin. Stir in the leeks, ginger, and garlic for 4 minutes. Place the vegetables in a bowl and serve.

Time 20 mins

Special Burger

The cabbage should be massaged for about 4 minutes in a large dish with 1 tablespoon of kosher salt, until it softens and releases its moisture. Rinse and drain the cabbage in a colander. Then select your preferred grilled patty and season it with Italian oregano. Combine everything, then choose your favorite dip like Caramelized Onion or ketchup.

Time 30 mins
